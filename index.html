Horizen Lounge

Cork, Horizen Lounge, and Jack London Restaurant, presented by the Horizen Group, are Shanghai’s most exclusive entertainment and dining venues. Horizen 集团为您精彩呈现上海最顶级的娱乐和餐饮享受-Cork, Horizen lounge 和 Jack London 餐厅

The Horizen experience begins with cork. Located at 1 Jinglingdong Road, facing the Huangpu River, cork is a tasting room and retail wine specialist, providing a comfortable setting to enjoy a diverse selection of wines and spirits. Patrons, and club members can enjoy wine tutorials, tastings and food pairings, and are able to make on premise purchases of bottles or place special orders for delivery. Horizen的Cork位于金陵东路1号，正对黄浦江，浦江美景尽收眼底 . 私密 舒适的 环境让您可以抛开喧嚣繁杂，静静享受美酒和闲暇时光。 客人和俱乐部的会员更 可以享受到红酒知识的辅导，顶级红酒品尝以及美酒和美食搭配的知识等，并且可 以根据客人的要求进行个性化的订购和送达。

Horizen Lounge, on the 23rd floor above cork, features premium wines and spirits, paired with distinct luxury offerings. With its warmth and refinement, it is the ideal environment for the pleasure of conversation and drinks. Guests entering the sumptuous luxury of the lounge are greeted with breathtaking sights. Every table presents unparalleled views of the Bund waterfront and the Pudong Lujiazui District. From classic cocktails for two, to superior bottle service for your parties’ entertainment, Horizen Lounge surpasses expectations with its commitment to each guest’s satisfaction. Horizen Lounge 位于Cork之上的23楼，顶级的的美酒香槟，极 致的美食和舒适的环境让您在此流连忘返。 奢华的潮流地标，炫目的外滩美景， 每张桌子都是直面浦东陆家嘴的一线江景。 从两个人的情调小酌到各式私人聚 会，Horizen Lounge都能为每位客人带来超乎想象的尊贵体验。

Upstairs from the lounge on the 24th floor is Jack London Restaurant. Sharing the same extraordinary views, it provides the elegant sophistication that is expected from Shanghai’s elite tastemakers. The culinary approach of Jack London is based on California Cuisine, a philosophy inspired by the local environment and the diverse cultures of Shanghai. It is a forward thinking approach, encompassing the meticulous execution of classical, progressive, and western culinary techniques. This style of dining allows the guest a diverse experience, with multiple courses that demonstrate a great variety of ingredients, and showcases their beauty with elegant presentations. The exquisite foods of Jack London Restaurant, prepared by Michelin starred Chef Anna Bautista, will be available throughout the night. 24楼是Jack London 餐厅。同样炫目的美景，米其林星级厨师Anna Bautista的极致料理正是上 海精英们的品味之选。Jack London餐厅的菜肴是基于加州美食的制作理念并融入 上海的国际氛围和海派文化。这个超前的理念，包含了经典菜肴的精挑细作，革新 以及西式菜肴精湛的烹饪技艺。丰富口感多层次的菜肴和精致的菜式呈现让您享受 到完全不同的美食体验。
